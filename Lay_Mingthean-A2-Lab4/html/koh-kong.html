Koh Kong Province

Koh Kong (Khmer: ខេត្តកោះកុង; IPA: [kɑh koŋ], "Kong Island") is a province (khaet) of Cambodia. The name means 'Kŏng Island Province'. Its capital is Khemarak Phoumin (Koh Kong). The province was called Patchan Khiri Khet Province (Thai: ปัจจันตคิรีเขตร; Thai pronunciation: [pàt.tɕan.tàʔ kʰi.riː.kʰèːt]) from 1795 to 1904.[3]
