High School Diploma at Hun Sen Bun Rany Wat Phnom

About this school, it named about our prime minister because he is the one who start and fund this school. Hun Sen Bun Rany Wat Phnom High School in Phnom Penh, Cambodia. Hun Sen Bun Rany Wat Phnom High School classified as Primary, Secondary & High School Public.

My Own Experience

For my own experience wehn I was study there, this school is the same as the same as other public school in Phnom Penh. The teaching method is not consider as top-notch but if the student is try hard in this school by study extra with anothe tutor, they might become outstanding. However, I think I am not try hard enough when I was in high school and luckily enough I pass the BACII exam.
