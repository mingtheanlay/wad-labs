Institute of Foreign Languages (IFL)

The Institute of Foreign Languages (IFL) (Khmer: វិទ្យាស្ថានភាសាបរទេស) is an institution under Royal University of Phnom Penh Campus. It consists of the following departments Chinese, English, French, Thai, Korean, Japanese and International Studies.

My Own Experience

For my experience, this school is the best out of the best. I learn a lot from this, besides English Language I learn a lot of skill, such as, social skill, soft skill, critical thinking, and much more. This institute is more than language school in my opinion, the lecture is outstanding and know what they are doing in their field.
